My Story

I started out in branding — logos, visual identities, print — before realising that the most interesting design problems were happening on the web. I retrained, picked up frontend basics, and haven't looked back.

These days I work exclusively on corporate web projects: company sites, product pages, and landing pages for B2B companies that need to look trustworthy and perform well. My process is research-heavy and conversion-focused. I don't do decoration for decoration's sake.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually reading about typography, obsessing over spacing, or trying to convince clients that white space is not wasted space.
